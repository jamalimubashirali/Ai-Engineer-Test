# Q4: Evaluate 3 AI image generation outputs for brand safety

_Scenario:_ We are evaluating AI-generated images for a family-friendly automotive brand's summer road trip campaign.

## Image Output 1: "Family packing an SUV at sunset"

- **Visuals:** A smiling family of four standing near the open trunk of a modern SUV. The lighting is golden hour. The background is a suburban driveway. However, upon close inspection, the father figure has six fingers on one hand, and the license plate displays gibberish that resembles a mild profanity (e.g., "SH1T-8X").
- **Brand Safety Score:** 3/10 (High Risk)
- **Explanation:** While the thematic content and overall composition perfectly match the family-friendly, optimistic tone of the campaign, the AI artifacting creates severe brand safety risks. The distorted hand (six fingers) looks uncanny and will immediately draw negative attention and mockery in social media comments. Worse, the hallucinatory license plate reading as a profanity is a critical brand safety violation for a family-oriented brand. This image cannot be used without heavy manual retouching.

## Image Output 2: "Car driving through a majestic mountain pass"

- **Visuals:** The brand's SUV driving dynamically around a curve in a lush alpine mountain pass. The vehicle looks polished and accurate to the make/model. The environment is breathtaking. There are no other cars or people visible.
- **Brand Safety Score:** 9/10 (Safe)
- **Explanation:** This image is extremely safe. It highlights the product in an aspirational, adventurous setting suitable for a summer road trip campaign. There are no AI artifacts visible on the car's geometry, and the lack of human subjects avoids the uncanny valley effect entirely. It aligns perfectly with standard automotive advertising tropes. The only reason it isn't a 10/10 is that it lacks the "family" element requested in the campaign brief, but from a strict _safety_ perspective, it is flawless.

## Image Output 3: "Teens hanging out of a car sunroof on the highway"

- **Visuals:** Two teenagers standing up through the sunroof of the SUV, cheering with their arms in the air while the car is clearly in motion on a highway. The driver is looking back at them, laughing, taking his eyes off the road.
- **Brand Safety Score:** 1/10 (Critical Risk - Unusable)
- **Explanation:** This is a catastrophic brand safety failure. The image depicts illegal, highly dangerous behavior (unrestrained passengers hanging out of a moving vehicle) and reckless driving (taking eyes off the road). For any automotive brand, promoting or associating with unsafe driving practices is an absolute red line that can lead to PR disasters and potential legal liability. This image must be flagged and discarded immediately, regardless of how aesthetically pleasing the lighting or rendering might be.
